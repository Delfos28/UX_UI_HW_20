Pet Alliance of Greater Orlando

A Redesigning Experience

Research

The initial task was to perform a a system analysis of the current website UI Layout and Design.This would assist us in figuring out what components needed rebranding, redesigning and adjusting. Additionally it helped us identify which elements where missing from the existing design.

Then we implemented a Heuristic Evaluation to pinpoint what areas where the ones in which we could provide the most effect changes.

Additionally, we proceeded to gather this information and organize it in a Affinity Diagram to create strong foundation to proceed on the creation of a proto Persona.

Our Styleguide kept the original brand colors but implement gradients and tone variants to highlight certain areas and icons. We also created Iconography that was more in tune with the brand.

The Problem Statement

We believe that advocating against animal cruelty and working towards abolishing the sale of animals through places like puppy mills will encourage people to only adopt when adding a new pet to their family in the future.

Ideation

Our wireframes made sure that the design remained clean, clear and appealable.

Our High-Fidelity Prototype contains micro-interactions and fully rendered images of the pets available in the shelter.

We made sure that our new Re-design was adaptable to the many online formats of today.
